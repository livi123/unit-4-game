Crystal Collector

Rules of The Game


  1. The computer will generate a random number that will appear under "Number To Get" in the left hand box above the crystals.
  2. You can click on the following crystals:
    • Blue Crystal
    • Green Crystal
    • Red Crystal
    • Yellow Crystal
  3. Each crystal you click will add to your Total Score.
  4. If the total you reach by randomly clicking on the crystals matches the Number To Get, you win the game.
  5. If however Your Total Score goes above the Number to Get, then you lose the game.
  6. The number that will be added to Your Total Score is generated by the computer and will be unknown to you. You will discover this number after clicking on a crystal.
